Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees and showcasing cutting-edge innovations in software, hardware, and digital transformation.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and education in the tech community. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to shape the future through technology and shared knowledge.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology:

  • Gbemi Odukoya – Celebrated for her pioneering work in computing theory and algorithmic design.
  • Seyi Victor – Known for his foundational contributions to computer science and artificial intelligence.
  • Tomi Philip – Renowned for her work on programming languages and software development.
